Fried egg

Simple fried eggs.

  • Eggs
  • Frying pan
  • Oil
  • Salt
  1. Pour a little bit of oil into the pan, just a spoonful of two.
  2. Crack the eggs into the pan without breaking the yolk.
  3. Salt the yolk and the whites for taste
  4. When the bottom part starts to get crispy, serve it.
